### The third Maine State Championship and Open will be on Sunday September 21st, 2025, at Think Tank in Portland.

**Who:**
A tournament open to all members of the [AGA](https://www.usgo.org/)!
Joining the AGA typically costs $30; if you're joining as a Maine resident, contact us to get a discount code.
The tournament is free for new AGA members.
The top performing Maine resident will be the new Maine State Champion.

**What:**
Three rounds of games, AGA rules,
20 minutes plus 20 seconds Fischer time.
Everyone is eligible to play all rounds.

<p style="padding-left: 2em;  padding-right: 3em;  line-height: 125%;">**Maine State Championship:** A knockout tournament for up to 8 of the highest ranked Maine residents. As players get knocked out, they join the Open tournament. The winner will be crowned Maine State Champion.</p>

<p style="padding-left: 2em;  padding-right: 3em;  line-height: 125%;">**Maine Open:** A [McMahon tournament](https://senseis.xmp.net/?McMahonPairing) open to all AGA members. As much as possible, players will play even games against opponents of a similar rank. There will be a prize for the top player.</p>

**Where:**

<p style="line-height: 125%;">[Think Tank Coworking](https://www.thinktankcoworking.com/locations)</p>
<p style="line-height: 125%;">[533 Congress St  
Portland, ME 04101](https://www.openstreetmap.org/directions?to=43.655940%2C+-70.261436#map=19/43.655931/-70.261905)</p>

On street parking should be available in the proximity with no charge for city metered
spaces (because it's Sunday).


**When:**
September 21st, 2025. Check-in will be from 9-9:45am; 
first games will start at 10am. We’ll finish up by 6pm.

**Why:**
A chance to meet and challenge Go players in the region as well as to take part in 
the prestigious 50-State Championship.
We’ll have coffee & snacks in the morning; there are lots of good choices for lunch in 
easy walking distance. Games will count toward AGA rankings.

**How:**
Please register in advance using the form below.

Registration for existing AGA members is $10, payable by
Venmo ([@Dyonn](https://account.venmo.com/u/Dyonn), phone ends in _-4753_),
Paypal ([jonathan.green.dyonn@gmail.com](https://paypal.me/JonathanGreenGo))
or when you check in that day.
